How Safety Flags Work

Safety flags are evaluated independently of the Gradient Boosting model. They are hard-coded clinical rules grounded in published oncology guidelines. When a flag is triggered, it is always surfaced to the clinician — regardless of what the ML probability score says.

IMMEDIATE flags → RED  override the GB output and force the RED band. These represent combinations of findings requiring same-day clinical action.

PROMPT flags → YELLOW  escalate the band to at least YELLOW. If the GB model already scores in the RED zone, the flag adds clinical context without downgrading the band.

Flags can only escalate — never downgrade. A LOW confidence GREEN can be forced to RED by a single IMMEDIATE flag.
